Daily Events (Rewards & Locations)

In: Uncategorized

Pokémon Unbound has dozens of events based on your device’s clock. Some events can only be initiated at a certain time, while others only happen on a certain day (daily or weekly).

Most of these events also yield rewards like rare berries or rare items that you can’t get elsewhere.

The table below lists every daily event in the game, its location, when you can initiate it, and the reward(s) you can get from each.

Note: This table does not include any of the Magnolia Café events. Those are listed on a separate Magnolia Café page here.
