Manectite

In: Mega Stones
Manectite (Mega Stone)
Location Found on Thundercap Mountain 3F, behind the electric fence. Accessible only after solving the pressure plate puzzle.
Mega Evolution Manectric

Prerequisites

  • Have access to Epidimy Town
  • Have Surf
  • Have Rock Smash
  • Have Strength

Location

From outside of the Epidimy Town Pokémon Center, make your way to the southeast corner of the town.

Enter Thundercap Mountain from the entrance you see here.

Follow the linear path north and then east.

You’ll come across a ladder you want to take to the upper floor.

Now, take the stairs directly east of you to get on a rocky platform and follow the path north.

Get off the platform from the other side and climb the stairs onto the east platform.

Continue following the path until you can turn south to cross the blue and white bridge.

Climb the ladder to go to a higher part of the mountain.

Follow the path south until you can’t go south anymore, at which point you want to start going east.

Climb the ladder at the end of the path to go to Thundercap Mountain’s fourth floor.

Climb the stairs leading to the south platform and follow the path going west.

Continue on the path until you see the boulder you want to push.

Push the boulder south until you drop it through the small hole in the ground, and then follow after the boulder by walking into the hole.

Now that you have a boulder for this puzzle, you have to set up the puzzle on the east side of the room.

To do this, climb the east stairs and take the ladder above you.

Slide down the south slope and follow the path west.

You’ll be able to go south after jumping over some ledges where you’ll see some smashable rocks.

Continue east after using Rock Smash on the rocks.

Continue east, even when you have to use Surf to continue on the path.

You’ll eventually see a ladder you want to take to go higher.

Walk north after climbing the ladder.

Solve the pressure plate puzzle by pushing a boulder on the plate using Strength.

Backtrack to the ladder after solving the puzzle, but instead of descending the ladder, you want to follow the path west.

Jump from the small ledge and continue on the path west.

You’ll shortly reach the previous pressure plate puzzle.

Push the boulder you set up before so it goes left, up, left up (see the image below). This will solve the final pressure plate puzzle.

Climb the east stairs again after pushing the boulder on the plate.

Follow the middle bridge going east

Descend from the stairs and follow the path west past the huge stone slab.

The Manectite is at the end of this path.
