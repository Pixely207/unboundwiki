TMs & HMs

In: TMs & HMs

This page contains a list of every TM & HM found in Pokemon Unbound. We’ve included the exact location and a screenshot for each one.

You can also click any TM/HM name in the tables below to visit a dedicated wiki page with more details on that specific TM or HM.

List of All HMs

HM Location Description Type Total PP Screenshot
HM01 Cut KBT Expressway

Given by a mountain climber NPC in the KBT Expressway South of Crater Town. 		A basic attack that can be used to cut down thin trees and grass. Normal 30
HM02 Fly Fallshore City – Outside the Gym

Given by Mel outside of the Fallshore City Gym after defeating him and exiting. 		A 2-turn move that hits the target on the second turn. Use it to fly to any known town. Flying 15
HM03 Surf Route 12

Given by your rival on Route 12 after acquiring the ability to Mega Evolve your Pokémon. 		A big wave crashes down on the foe. Can also be used for crossing waters. Water 15
HM04 Strength Epidimy Town – West-most house

Given by the NPC inside of the West-most house in Epidimy Town. 		The foe is slugged at maximum power. Can also be used to move boulders. Normal 15
HM05 Dive Seaport City – Captain Davy’s Shipyard

After beating the Elite Four and becoming champion, go to Captain Davy’s Shipyard in Seaport City and buy the ADM from him for 250,000 Pokémon Dollars. He’ll give you the HM for Dive too. 		The user dives underwater the first turn and attacks the next turn. Water 10
HM06 Rock Smash Blizzard City – North-most house

Given by the Mountain Climber NPC in Blizzard City. You can find him on the ground floor of the house east of the KBT Expressway entrance. 		An attack that lowers defense. It can also be used to smash cracked boulders. Fighting 15
HM07 Waterfall Polder Town – Gym

Given as a reward for defeating Tessy at the Polder Town Gym. 		A powerful charge attack. It can also be used to climb a waterfall. Water 15
HM08 Rock Climb Route 17

Given by the construction worker NPC on Route 17. 		A charging attack that may confuse the foe. Can also be used to scale walls. Rock 20
