1F (East Side)

Cinder Volcano is a vast cave complex with several floors, so it’s a little easy to get lost or turned around in here.

You’ve got to get to the top of the volcano to stop the Shadow from capturing Moltres though, so you better hurry.

Items
Rawst Berry
(hidden)
Zygarde Cell
Rare Candy

Walk north and you’ll see a ladder leading up to the second floor.

To your left is a passage that is currently blocked by a rock, but when you’re done in Cinder Volcano it will be gone and act as a shortcut between the two sections of the cave.

You’ll find yourself on an island with a Trainer Tips signpost on it. North of here is a ladder that takes you back down to the third floor for an optional trainer and a TM.

5F (Summit)

When you make it to the fifth floor of the volcano – which is also its summit – you’ll see that you’re far too late to stop the Shadow.

When the cutscene finishes, walk northwest and you’ll see that someone else is about to confront the Shadow.

It’s Arthur’s grandson, Jax.

Jax attempts to stop Ivory, the member of the Shadow that captured Moltres, from leaving.

However, Marlon – the one who captured Articuno – arrives and him and Ivory just manage to get away.

It’s at that point that Jax asks to team up with you.

This is optional – you can refuse his help if you want. If you accept it, the next few battles with trainers will be Double Battles (if they have two Pokémon or more).

When you’ve made your decision, go to the ladder and climb it down to the fourth floor – but this time you’ll be on the west side of the volcano.

Once you’ve dealt with Foster – which should not take long since he only has one Pokémon – walk south and you’ll find Hiker Clark.

After beating Hiker Clark, continue the winding and rocky path through the lava fields until you reach the final area of this floor.

Kinder Axle is the last trainer you can beat with Jax – after that, he decides to rush ahead to confront the Shadow first.

Tip: The Chansey near the ladder will heal your Pokémon, which will come in handy considering the next battle you’ll be fighting.

1F (West Side) – Shadow Battle

Jax is already battling Marlon, but they appear to be at a stalemate when you arrive.

Items
Super Potion

Regardless of whether you accepted his help earlier, Jax will join you for one final battle against Marlon and a Shadow Grunt.

You’ll be asked to select three Pokémon you’ll use in the battle, and Jax will supply the other three – a Staravia, a Magnemite, and a Golett.

Together with Jax, you manage to beat the Shadow goons. However, Moltres has already been captured and Ivory has escaped, so it’s a bit of a hollow victory.

After the Shadow leave the cave, Jax brings you back to Arthur’s house in Dresco Town.

Arthur and Professor Log tell you that the only bird remaining is Zapdos, and it lives on Thundercap Mountain.

Jax will go and investigate a way to stop the Shadow with a mysterious item, and you will continue to Crater Town – which is on the way to Thundercap.

Professor Log decides to go home and tell your mother you’re continuing the Pokémon League challenge, which you technically still are. There’s a Gym in Crater Town – and an opportunity to get your second badge.

After the meeting, Jax offers you a lift back to the western exit of Cinder Volcano. If you don’t want to go with him right now he’ll give you 8 Repels.

Route 4 is broken in half by Cinder Volcano, and the west side of the caverns naturally leads to the west side of Route 4.

There’s a Porygon near the cave entrance that will act as Pokémon Storage if you need it.

This side of Route 4 is fairly quiet, with a few optional trainers for you to beat and items to collect. One thing you won’t fail to notice is a Lapras, docked on the south side of a bridge.

This Lapras is the only way for us to get to Route 5, since we don’t have the HM Waterfall yet.

So once you’ve gotten everything in order, hop on the Lapras’ back and it will take you where you need to go.
