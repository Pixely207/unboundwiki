Useful NPCs

In: Uncategorized

This page offers a curated list of important NPCs in the world of Pokémon Unbound. These are NPCs that you can go to for acquiring rare items, or for getting certain services performed like IV/EV edits, move reminder services, or many other similar features.

Note: If you’re looking for a list of all move tutors then we have a separate page for those NPCs.
NPC What they do Location Screenshot
IV Changer Increases or maximizes the IV of a stat (or all stats) on any Pokemon, in exchange for bottle caps or gold bottle caps respectively. Standing west of the Seaport City Port.
EV Changer Maximizes or purges one of your Pokémon’s EVs in exchange for Battle Points. Standing behind the eastern-most stall in the main Battle Frontier area.
Nature Changer Changes a Pokémon’s nature in exchange for Battle Points. There are two different Nature Changers in the game:

  1. Inside of the house southwest of the Tehl Town Pokemon Center
  2. Behind the stall in the top-left corner of the Battle Frontier
Move Relearner Allows a Pokemon to relearn any forgotten move in exchange for a Heart Scale. Inside of the house south of the Crater Town Pokemon Center.
Move Deleter Allows you to delete any of your Pokémon’s moves, including HM ones. Inside of the house northwest of the Epidimy Town Pokemon Center.
Egg Move Tutor Teaches a Pokemon its specific egg moves in exchange for Battle Points. Standing behind the stall west of the Battle Frontier entrance.
Egg Move Transfer Tutor Transfers egg moves between two Pokémon of the same evolutionary line. Inside the Day Care Center.
Name Rater Rates & renames a Pokémon’s nickname. Inside the south-most house in Tehl Town.
Happiness Checker Checks the happiness of the first Pokemon in the player’s party. Inside of the house directly south of the Rift Cave entrance.
Happiness Increaser Plays with the first Pokemon in the player’s party to increase its overall happiness. Inside of the house directly south of the Rift Cave entrance.
Egg Lady (Magnolia Cafe) Gives away and sells eggs. Occasionally sits outside the Magnolia Town Cafe depending on the time and day.
Egg Buyer Buys eggs from the player for 2,000 Poke Dollars per egg. Inside of the house west of the Redwood Village Gym.
Egg Hatcher Significantly speeds up the hatching speed of all eggs in the player’s party for 12,500 Poke Dollars. Inside of the house west of the Redwood Village Gym.
Hidden Power Changer Changes the select Pokemon’s Hidden Power in exchange for Battle Points. Standing behind a stall south of the main Battle Frontier building.
Incense Seller Sells every available incense in the game. On the top floor of the Dehara City Mall. Only available in the post-game.
Ore Maniac Purchases all types of ores from the player. Inside of the house directly east of the Dehara City Gym.
Food Maniac Purchases all food items and berries from the player. Standing in the very south side of the Dehara City Mall’s second floor.
Relic Maniac Purchases relics and other rare items from the player. Inside of the house directly behind the Gurun Town Pokemon Center.
Rare Bone Maniac Purchases rare bones from the player for 10,000 Poke Dollars. Inside of the house directly behind the Gurun Town Pokemon Center.
Plate Maker Gives all types of plates in exchange for shards. Inside Captain Davy’s Shipyard in Seaport City.
Master Ball Maker Gives you a Master Ball in exchange for thirty (30) Gems of any type. Standing behind the southwest stall in the Cube Corp. area.
Poke Ball Changer Changes the Poke Ball type of any selected Pokemon, in exchange for 1 Ring Target. Standing behind the stall directly west of the main Cube Corp. building.
Beast Ball NPC Creates Beast Balls in exchange for Iron Balls. Standing behind the northeast stall in the Cube Corp. area.
Hard Stone to Gem NPC Gives the player any type of Gem in exchange for 20 Hard Stones. Must complete the “As Hard as They Come” side mission first. Inside of the house directly behind the Gurun Town Pokemon Center.
Unova Starters lady Gives players the Unova starters. You will be able to obtain progressively more starters from this NPC as you acquire more badges. Inside the west-most house of Route 5, just west of the Day Care Center.
Togepi Egg Lady Gives the player a Togepi egg. Inside the house directly east of the Fallshore City Poke Mart.
Sinnoh Starters lady Gives players the Sinnoh starters. You will be able to obtain progressively more starters from this NPC as you acquire more badges. Inside of the house southeast of the Rift Cave entrance.
Alola Starters lady Gives players the Alola starters. You will be able to obtain progressively more starters from this NPC as you acquire more badges. Inside of the house west of the Seaport City KBT Expressway entrance.
Macho Brace upgrade NPC Gives the player a Macho Brace and upgrades it in exchange for Everstones. Standing next to a tree near the south-most waterfall on Route 10, just south of the Cave of Being. Requires Surf.
Power Items upgrade NPC Upgrades the player’s power items in exchange for Star Pieces. East of Antisis City but outside Thundercap Mountain.
Lucky Egg upgrade NPC Upgrades the player’s Lucky Eggs in exchange for Big Pearls. Must complete the “Exp. Millionaire” side mission first. Inside of the Fallshore City Mission HQ.
Amulet Coin upgrade NPC Upgrades your Amulet Coin and Luck Incense items in exchange for Big Nuggets. Must show the NPC Regigigas first. Standing in the center of Tehl Town.
Fossil Reviver Revives any fossil the player may have in their inventory. Inside the house east of the Gurun Town KBT Expressway entrance.
Evolution Stone Creator Creates any type of evolution stone in exchange for 4 Everstones. Directly east of the main Cube Corp. building.
