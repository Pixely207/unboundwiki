Below is a list of every move tutor in Unbound, along with their locations, the moves they teach, and what each move costs.

You can use table of contents above to jump to a specific tutor, or you can use CTRL+F to search the page for a specific move to find what you’re looking for.

Shard Tutors

A handful of tutors scattered around the game teach players certain moves in exchange for specific types of shards. The shard’s color is directly related to the type of move players learn from the tutor.

Blue Shard Tutor

Location Found on the ground floor of the first house when entering Blizzard City from Route 8.
Screenshot
Unlock Requirements Reach Blizzard City
Blue Shard Tutor – Moves List
Move Cost
Ice Punch 2 Blue Shards

Green Shard Tutor

Location Found inside the house directly behind the Vivill Town Pokémon Mart.
Screenshot
Unlock Requirements Clear the Vivill Town Team Shadow main storyline.
Green Shard Tutor – Moves List
Move Cost
Seed Bomb 2 Green Shards

Yellow Shard Tutor

Location Found on the second floor of the house southeast of Dehara City’s Rift Cave. Speak with the yellow-haired NPC next to the tutor to get 2 Yellow Shards for free.
Screenshot
Unlock Requirements Reach Dehara City.
Yellow Shard Tutor – Moves List
Move Cost
Thunder Punch 2 Yellow Shards

Red Shard Tutor

Location Found inside of the house west of the Tarmigan Town Pokemon Center.
Screenshot
Unlock Requirements Reach Tarmigan Town.
Red Shard Tutor – Moves List
Move Cost
Fire Punch 2 Red Shards

KBT Expressway Gem Family Move Tutors

The KBT Expressway is home to the Gem Family, a group of trainers who teach players a specific move in exchange for a set number of gems. The moves they teach change depending on the day, and every trainer has a designated typing.

Most move tutors in Pokemon Unbound are in the Battle Frontier, an end-game area. Every tutor here takes BP, or Battle Points as payment, which can be obtained by participating in the main Battle Frontier dome.
