Route 2

In: Locations

Route 2, or ‘Ocean Lane’, is located on the other side of Icicle Cave from Route 1, and leads directly to Dresco Town. There are plenty of trainers here for you to sharpen your skills on.

There’s also a water connection with Route 15 and Vivill Town, if you have Surf.
