Ruins of Void

In: Locations

The Ruins of Void is an ancient complex at the north end of Route 14, and you are first teleported here by Hoopa during the main story to confront the leader of the Light of Ruin, Aklove. After you become Pokémon Champion there are further secrets to discover here, including an underwater area and a final rematch with Aklove.
