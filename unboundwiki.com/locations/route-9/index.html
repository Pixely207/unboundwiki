Route 9

In: Locations

Route 9, also known as “Autumnal Woods” or Atul Woods, consists mostly of autumn-colored trees and long grass fields surrounding a large lake.

When arriving here first during the main story, the area is occupied by Shadow Grunts. Once their leader is beaten in the Secluded House, regular trainers will return to the area to be challenged.

For the Route 9 Raid Den details please visit this page.
