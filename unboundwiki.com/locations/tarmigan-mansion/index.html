Tarmigan Mansion

In: Locations

Tarmigan Mansion is a dungeon in Pokémon Unbound. When you beat it, you’ll gain the power of Mega Evolution for yourself and your Pokémon, and this location is essentially a Pokémon Gym in everything but name.

There are no wild Pokémon in its halls, but there are plenty of secrets, items and trainers to discover.
