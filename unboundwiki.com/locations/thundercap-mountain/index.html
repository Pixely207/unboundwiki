Thundercap Mountain

In: Locations

Thundercap Mountain is a massive and sprawling cave complex that is possibly one of the largest indoor dungeons in Pokémon Unbound.

Whole sections of it are gated off behind HM requirements, so in a regular playthrough you will be returning here multiple times.
