Dehara City

In: Locations

Dehara City is the largest city in Borrius, and is the home of the Electric-type Gym with leader Galavan. It is a key location in the story of the potential Pokemon Champion and there is a lot to see, do, and collect here.

The Dehara Department Store is full of deals and trainers to beat, and the Double Battle House will provide a challenge. Once business here is concluded, you’ll need a set of Go-Go Goggles to head east across the Great Desert.
