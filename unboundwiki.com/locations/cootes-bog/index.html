Cootes Bog

In: Locations

Cootes Bog is a large stretch of wetland on East Borrius, north of Polder Town and south of Magnolia Town and Magnolia Field. There are a lot of land and water Pokémon to catch here, and this is where you’ll use the HM Waterfall to access Crystal Peak.
