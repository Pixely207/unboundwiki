Mission #059: Swords of Justice

In: Missions
Mission #059: Swords of Justice
Start Location Fallshore City, Mission HQ 2F – Black Belt
Unlock Requirements
  • Become Pokémon Champion
  • Complete 20 Missions and receive Type:Null from the Pokémon Ranger
Objectives Capture the three Swords of Justice
Reward Item: 10 Fast Balls
Event: Keldeo Capture Opportunity

In the Fallshore Mission HQ, after completing 20 Missions and receiving Type: Null, a Black Belt will ask a favor of you.

If you travel to Victory Road and then capture the Swords of Justice Virizion, Terrakion and Cobalion, he’ll give you 10 Fast Balls. In addition, you’ll be able to capture Keldeo.

Starting the Mission

The Mission HQ is on the west side of Fallshore City just north of the Marketplace.

Note: In order to receive this mission, you need to have become Pokémon Champion and already completed 20 other missions. You’ll also need to have picked up the reward for that from the Pokémon Ranger near the inside doorway of Mission HQ, which is a Type: Null.

Go upstairs and into the apartment – there’s a Black Belt at the table in front of the kitchen.

He tells you that the Swords of Justice are on Victory Road, and you’ll need to head there to catch them.

How to Complete

Fly to Victory Road and you’ll be in front of the Pokémon Center on Route 18. From there, go northwest to the Victory Road entrance.

Head inside to the first floor of Victory Road.

Then cross the bridge, go down the stairs and surf east to the ladder up to the second floor.

From the ladder, go north and west.

You’ll be at the cavern exit out into the hedge maze. Go through it.

Virizion

The first of the Swords of Justice we encounter on Victory Road is Virizion, and it’s somewhere in the hedge maze.

It’s in the central grass patch, north of the Kindler and Bug Maniac Double Battle trainers.

Approach it, and it’ll run away.

Each member of the Swords of Justice will need to be found several times in order for them to appear at their catching location, and they don’t stray far, sticking to whatever level they’re found on.

Next, go to the very northeast point of the maze and you’ll find Virizion in the dead end.

It will then flee directly west to the northwest corner just outside the maze.

After that, it will run to the entrance to the maze.

When you interact with it this time, it will leave the floor completely and go to the 3F cave.

Go north to the 3F entrance at the other end of the maze and head on inside.

Terrakion

As soon as you enter the cave you’ll see Terrakion directly in front of you in that little dead end. Talk to it, and it will run away to its next location on this floor.

Head west and then north to the central part of the cavern.

When you go through this area, you’ll see Virizion has appeared around the central rock. Don’t interact with it when it’s here, or it will run back to the hedge maze. When all three Swords of Justice retreat to this rock, they’ll be able to be captured.

For now, go past it to the east section of the cavern.

Terrakion will be hiding here – talk to it and it will run off to the southwest.

After finding it there, it will then move to the cave exit in the northwest.

When you approach Terrakion this time, he’ll go to the central rock with Virizion.

With two of the three Swords of Justice in place, go back to the snowy ascent cavern exit.

Go outside into the snow, and you’ll need to track down Cobalion.

Cobalion

Once outside in the cold, go east to where Kyurem’s Cave is.

This is the first location you’ll find Cobalion. Interact with it and it’ll run off to the southwest.

Then, it will run off to the grassy cliff in the northeast.

After that, it will appear in the central rocky area.

Interact with the Cobalion one final time here and it will go off to join the others.

Catching the Swords

Once all three are in place, you’re free to catch the Swords of Justice one by one. They’re extremely difficult to catch and it’s probably best done with Dusk Balls or Timer Balls.

Virizion
Grass/Fighting
(Lv. 50)
Terrakion
Rock/Fighting
(Lv. 50)
Cobalion
Steel/Fighting
(Lv. 50)

When you’ve got all three, go to the nearest Pokémon Center and put them in your party. Then, return to the Black Belt in Fallshore City.

Show the three Swords of Justice to him, and he’ll tell you to meet him somewhere on Route 17.

Fly to Seaport City and head west.

Surf west until you’re down the bottom of the middle island, on a secluded beach.

When you talk to the Black Belt, the Swords of Justice combine their power to open a cave in the cliff wall.

The Black Belt will end the mission and reward you with 10 Fast Balls, telling you they’ll help with catching the Pokémon inside.

You’ll get an opportunity to catch the other Sword of Justice, Keldeo.

Keldeo
Water/Fighting
(Lv. 80)
