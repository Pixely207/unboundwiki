Mission #066: The Endless Nightmare

In: Missions
Mission #066: The Endless Nightmare
Start Location Tarmigan Town, House next to Poke Mart – Sailor
Unlock Requirements
  • Become Pokémon Champion
Objectives Help a little boy with bad dream issues
Reward Item: Member Card

Speak to the sailor in the house next to the Poke Mart in Tarmigan Town, and he’ll ask you to cure his son of his perpetual bad dreams.

If you manage to do so, you’ll get a Member Card for the Tarmigan Inn.

Starting the Mission

Fly to Tarmigan Town and you’ll arrive in front of the Pokémon Center. From there, go north.

You’ll see a Poke Mart, and a house next to it.

Go inside the house and you’ll find a family in distress.

Their little boy has been suffering from bad dreams and can’t wake up. His father asks for your help, and needs someone to go to the Dream Lab to get a scientist to investigate.

How to Complete

Head to the Tarmigan Dream Lab, which is in the northwest corner of town.

The scientist you need to speak to is just east of the large table, near a Munna. She’ll agree to help the family.

She’ll head to the house, and once you’re all there she says that there may be only one thing that can help, and that’s a Lunar Wing from Fullmoon Island.

Luckily, the boy’s father Eldon is a sailor and has a boat, so he offers to take you there immediately.

Go to the Fallshore City dock, which is in the southwest part of town.

Once there, speak to Eldon and he’ll whisk you away to the island.

You’ll land on a small island with not much on it.

Go north and then east until you come to a forest.

Go on inside, and you’ll come to a sheltered glade.

This is the home of the Legendary Pokémon Cresselia, and you’ll need to defeat it to get the Lunar Wing.

It’s a Dynamax Battle, which means you’ll need to send out two Pokémon.

Legendary Pokémon
Dynamax
Cresselia
Psychic
(Lv. 60)

When Cresselia is defeated and captured, it will drop the Lunar Wing you need.
 

Go back to Eldon and the boat, and he’ll take you back to Fallshore City.

When you return, Eldon will head off first and you can meet him at his house.

Give the Lunar Wing to Eldon’s son, and he’ll be free of the nightmare.

However, it will then give one to you.

You’ll suddenly be trapped in a black void with only your reflection to look at.

Head north, where you’ll see someone familiar.

Speak to your mother over and over again, and she’ll just keep repeating that you’re home in a creepy way. She’ll disappear eventually, and you can walk east.

You’ll see Jax and Professor Log, or something that looks like them. Speak to Jax first and he’ll disappear.

Then, speak to Professor Log, and he’ll ask you for his package before disappearing as well.

Walk west, and you’ll see your Rival.

But he’s not himself, and he’s unconscious. Go east, and you’ll run into his father.

Marlon also has some creepy things to say to you, and when he’s gone continue east to find the boy.

He’s not himself either. Go northwest, and you’ll finally see Eldon.

Eldon will scream at you and then disappear.

Then, something else arrives.

Darkrai arrives, and is the Pokémon causing the whole nightmare to happen.

It will challenge you to a battle, and you’ll have to send out two Pokémon to fight it in Dynamax form. You won’t be able to catch it though just yet.

Dynamax Battle
Dynamax
Darkrai
Dark
(Lv. 75)

When Darkrai has been defeated, you’ll wake up from the nightmare and the boy will be fine.

Eldon will give you the Tarmigan Inn Member Card, which you can use to enter the Inn and face Darkrai for the final time, after which you can capture it.
