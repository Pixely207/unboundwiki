Mission #013: Basic Literacy

In: Missions
Mission #013: Basic Literacy
Start Location Dresco Town – man in beanie on southern tip
Unlock Requirements
  • Completed main story missions up until Dresco Town
Objectives Read all 45 Trainer Tips signs in the game
Reward Item: Notebook

A young aspiring punk rocker who can’t read wants you to demonstrate your reading skills by finding and reading all 45 Trainer Tips signs across Borrius. Doing so will get you the Notebook, an item required to upgrade the Stat Scanner into a mobile Move Tutor.

Starting the Mission

South of Jax and Arthur’s house in Dresco Town, on the southern tip, is a man who has a reading problem.

He wants you to read all the Trainer Tips signs in Borrius – there are 45 of them, and they are spread all over Borrius.

You can accept this mission at any time, but you will not be able to complete this mission until after beating the Pokémon League, because only then will Redwood Forest and Cube Corp. be available.

How to Complete

As you read Trainer Tip signs, their number and location will be recorded in the Mission Log so you can remember which ones you’ve read before and how many you are yet to find.
