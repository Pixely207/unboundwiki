All Berries

In: Items

Pokémon Unbound has multiple berries that players can obtain through methods like completing raids, stealing from wild Pokémon, and by purchasing them from certain vendors. The table below lists every berry in the game, alongside its use and acquisition methods.

If you’re looking for a specific berry then you can use CTRL+F or use your mobile browser’s “find in page” feature to look up a berry by name.

Tip: Aside from the listed methods, players can also acquire berries from key NPCs who give a set number of random berries as daily events.
